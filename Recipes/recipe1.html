Nana's Famous Grilled Chicken

Image credit: iFoodReal.com

The Story

I grew up eating my Nana's recipes, but her marinated chicken has always been my favorite. Crispy, juicy, savory, and a bit tangy, the perfect bite of chicken. Pairs well with starches like rice, pasta, and gnocci. Simply marinate at least 2 hours before cooking, though an overnight marinate shows the best results. Grill on a charcoal grill or pan sear it, whatever is avaliable to you! What makes this recipe great is it's flexibility.

The most tender and flavorful chicken you'll ever have! A staple of our family gatherings! - Nana



The Recipe

Ingredients
Ingredient Quantity Prep Time
Skin-On Chicken Thighs 4 Minimum 2 hrs
Lemon Juice 4 Oz 0
Garlic 3 Cloves 5 min
Vegetable Oil 16 Oz 0
Worcestershire Sauce 4 Oz 0
Herb Blend 12 Oz 5 min
Serves 4

The Herb Blend

Equal parts salt, pepper, oregano, thyme, onion powder, paprika, and cumin. Incorporate well into the marinade, you could even coat the chicken in this mix before putting it on the stove or grill for extra flavor!

Instructions

  1. Combined all ingredients to create marinade. Submerge chicken completely and let marinate.
  2. Marinate chicken for at least two hours, or overnight for best results.
  3. Grill on high heat for 5 minutes on each side.
  4. If desired, cook for an extra 2 minutes flipping every 30 seconds for crispy skin.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
